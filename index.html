About Us

Advanced Technologies (A-Tech) Education Centre is a Degree And Diploma Engineering Coaching Acadmey In Dahod, Gujarat, India with a focus on integrating technology with academics for engineering students in degree And diploma colleges. As of Now, This Academy is ranked #1 in the Dahod city of gujarat. This centre was founded in 2009 by Mr.Abbas Tukiwala and after that his brother was joined this centre and gave fully supports to build up this centre. Coaching Education For All Semester of All Branches such as Computer, Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, Electronics And Communication (EC) are conducting here. Specially Mathamatics for all semester includes Maths-1 to Maths-4 are conducting very efficiently and Regularly. Up to Current Year, there are approximately 1000 students get coaching from this centre and build their better future.

Apart From This, these centre offers many computer related courses such as Tally, Autocad, 3D Max, Micromedia Flash, D.T.P, Programming Languages and Long Term Courses such as DCA, PGDCA etc. This Centre developed Academy Related Projects in Computer, Electrical, E.C And Mechanical Engineering and provides final year Trainiing Programme For Studnets. This centre prepares and implements Disseartation related or research related topics during 2 years.This centre focuses on Computer and technology related study fields.
